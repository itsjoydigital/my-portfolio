About Me

I make social media less overwhelming for businesses.

I'm Joy, a Social Media Manager with 2+ years of experience across various niches, from jewelry and food to fitness, hygiene, and digital marketing. That range taught me how to adapt fast and create content that fits different industries.

Before I make a single graphic or write a single caption, I take time to understand the brand first. The voice, the audience, the goals. That's what makes the content actually work instead of just filling a feed.

Right now I'm focused on helping B2B brands build a strong social media presence, but I'm open to working with different types of clients. If you think we're a good fit, let's talk.
